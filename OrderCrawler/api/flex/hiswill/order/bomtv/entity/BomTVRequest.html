InheritanceBomTVRequest Entity Object
Implements IGridEntity

Descriptions of customer's requirements.

Print order system in Bom-TV, there's not any form and template pre-defined. So when customer orders their prints, they describes what they want by text sentences. BomTVRequest is the entity archiving those narrations.

Definition of task procedure about the Bom-TV order, a clerk of Red-Printing looks those descriptions by their own eyes and the clerk selects the matched products, materials, post-proceses and another related information on OrderCrawler. It's the instrinsic purpose and strategy of this OrderCrawler.
