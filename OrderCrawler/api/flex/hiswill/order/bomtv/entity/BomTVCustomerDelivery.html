An address of last client.

Within the framework of Red-Printing, orders from Bom-TV does not delivered to the last client but to the headquarter of the Bom-TV corporation. Because of this reason, BomTVOrder.delivery follows BomTVContractor.address

However, when BomTVDelivery.method is direct, the order's result have to be delievered to the final customer. So the delivery information of final customer must be managed by a extra, specified entity. It's the BomTVCustomerDelivery.
